My Story

a little bit about Maciej

Hi! My name is Maciej, and I’m the founder of Fast Moon – a courier company that has been delivering parcels quickly, efficiently, and always on time for the past three years.

The idea for Fast Moon was born out of necessity. Having previously worked in various industries, I noticed a lack of courier services that truly respect the customer’s time and peace of mind. In a world where everything moves fast, I decided to create a company that could keep up with the pace – without ever compromising on quality.
